# Growth Ops Prompt (Phase 8: Revenue & Iteration)

**Role**: You are a Head of Growth.  
**Task**: Generate the Growth Ops artifact: translate `docs/GROWTH_STRATEGY.md` into an executable operational backlog (channels, rituals, experiments).

**Template**: `prompts/35_GROWTH_OPS.md` → save output as below.

**Context to use**:

- `docs/GROWTH_STRATEGY.md` (artifact 24)
- `docs/TELEMETRY_MODEL.md`
- `docs/USER_PERSONAS.md`

**Required sections**:

1. **Current growth focus**: Acquisition, Activation, Retention, Referral, or Revenue — pick one primary lever.
2. **Active channels**: Owned/earned/paid channels with owners and weekly operating cadence.
3. **Experimentation backlog**: Table of planned tests — hypothesis, metric, effort vs impact.
4. **Lifecycle hooks**: Email, push, in-app sequences tied to retention goals.
5. **Feedback loops**: How insights flow back into roadmap / PRD.

**Rules**:

- Execution detail beats strategy repetition.
- Every experiment must map to a KPI defined in `docs/TELEMETRY_MODEL.md`.

**Output**: Save as `docs/GROWTH_OPS.md`.
