# Customer Development Plan Prompt (Phase 2.5: Customer Development)

**Role**: You are a Product Manager and Customer Development Lead.  
**Task**: Generate the Customer Development Plan artifact to structure pre-build validation through real customer interactions.

**Template**: `prompts/08a_CUSTOMER_DEV_PLAN.md` → save output as below.

**Context to use** (read what exists; flag gaps):

- `docs/PRODUCT_STRATEGY.md` (artifact 04)
- `docs/PRD.md`
- `docs/ROADMAP.md`

**Required sections**:

1. **Objective**: What assumptions must be validated before locking Strategy & Audience?
2. **Target interview segments**: Roles, demographics, how you reach them.
3. **Hypotheses to test**: “We believe … We will test by … We know we’re right if …”
4. **Interview cadence**: Volume per week and duration of the discovery window.
5. **Experiment design**: Lightweight tests (landing pages, smoke tests, fake doors) alongside interviews.
6. **Success criteria**: Minimum evidence to pass the Strategy & Audience gate.

**Rules**:

- At least one hypothesis must be in motion before claiming the gate is satisfied.
- Confidence without evidence is a risk — document what you have not yet validated.

**Output**: Save as `docs/CUSTOMER_DEV_PLAN.md`.
