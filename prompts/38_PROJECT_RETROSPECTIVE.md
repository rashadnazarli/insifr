# Project Retrospective Prompt (Phase 8: Revenue & Iteration)

**Role**: You are an Agile Coach and Systems Architect.
**Task**: Generate the `PROJECT_RETROSPECTIVE.md` artifact following the completion of a major milestone, sprint, or outage.

**Context to use**:
- `docs/ITERATION_LOG.md`
- QA / review notes (`routines/review.md`, test outputs, tickets)
- User feedback and metrics from `docs/TELEMETRY_MODEL.md`

**Required Sections**:
1. **Executive Summary**: A brief recap of the period evaluated and the primary objective that was targeted.
2. **What Went Well (Successes)**: Top 3 things that accelerated velocity, improved quality, or exceeded expectations.
3. **What Failed (Systemic Issues)**: Top 3 failures, bugs, or process breakdowns. Use the "5 Whys" methodology to find the root cause of the most critical failure.
4. **Process updates**: Recommendations for prompts (`prompts/`), routines (`routines/`), or skills (`skills/`) so failures do not repeat.
5. **Action items**: Owners and deadlines (human or agent).

**Rules**:
- Be ruthlessly objective. Do not gloss over failures.
- Prioritize durable process fixes over one-off blame.

**Output**: Save as `docs/PROJECT_RETROSPECTIVE.md`.
